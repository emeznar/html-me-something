Pebble Watchfaces

Pebble has some really interesting smart watches currently on the market and the direction they are taking in future development appears to be very promising. What sets them apart from other smart watches is that they have incorporated an open source concept into their products meaning any function can be customized whithin the limits of the hardware. Instead of having drag and drop watchface apps that allow you to "customize" the look of a watch, Pebble has a cloud development site that lets you upload code creating a watchface completely from a blank screen. With a little C code and javascript, anything is possible! Some of the watchfaces I have created are listed below. Feel free to take a look at them and if you are interested in them, click the link at the bottom of the page for more information.

  • Sports Related
  • Cool or rugged
  • A little lighter (not so tough)

Sports Related

Sports related wathfaces have a photo that is related to a mascot. They display 24hr time, date in all number format, and track steps, battery life, and weather.

Cool or Rugged

These watchfaces are cool with a little more rugged feel. They tend to have a more masculine theme, but would still be considered unisex. They display time in 24hr format and also display date, step count, battery life, and weather.

Lighter ‑ not so tough

These watchfaces have a lighter feel with lighter colors and would definitely be considered unisex. Some display time in 24hr format while others include 12hr format with AM or PM. Like the others, these watchfaces display date and track step count, battery life, and weather.
